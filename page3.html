Goals

            
            To promote knowledge sharing and exchange between Nordic and Nigerian stakeholders.

            To build the capacity of individuals and institutions in Nigeria to drive development.

            To support the development of sustainable solutions to Nigeria's development challenges.

            To foster collaborative partnerships between Nordic and Nigerian stakeholders.
            
            To contribute to the achievement of the Sustainable Development Goals in Nigeria.
